A lot of people have seemed to hate this movie but if you just turn your brain off and enjoy the ride its actually very enjoyable. It is very intense and epic in every way. John Cusack does a good job as well does the rest of the cast. Some of the parts are in this movie are pretty silly but its still fun.

This movie is about a man who starts hearing about how the world is going to end. At first he does not believe it but starts to see signs and then as the beginning of the end of the world starts to happen he struggles to keep him and his family safe.
